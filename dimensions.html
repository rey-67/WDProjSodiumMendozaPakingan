SurviveBlocks: Dimensions

The Overworld

The Overworld is the main dimension where players begin their journey. It contains a wide variety of biomes such as plains, forests, deserts, mountains, and oceans. Most resources like wood, iron, diamonds, villages, and animals are found here. Players build bases, explore caves, trade with villagers, and prepare gear for other dimensions.

The Nether

The Nether is a dangerous, hell-like dimension filled with lava, fire, and hostile mobs. Unique biomes such as Crimson Forests, Warped Forests, and Soul Sand Valleys exist here. Players explore the Nether to collect resources like glowstone, nether wart, and ancient debris, and to find Nether Fortresses for blaze rods.
